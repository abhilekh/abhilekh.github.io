    • To be Mr

      Life is what happens when you are busy planning other things. And that is exactly how it happened. While I was busy making plans to move out of Houston for a change, he swooped right in my life and I am gladly stuck in this city for the perfect reason!

      I enjoyed his company but a distinct memory where he indeed struck a chord of my heart was when I fell sick and while chatting with him one evening casually mentioned that and even without  me asking, he was at my doorstep an hour later with dinner and medicines!!

      His calmness and maturity brings the much needed balance in my hyper personality. And he still manages to be equally funny despite being responsible.

       

      And although he does operate on Pacific Time Zone living in Texas! :D, I am so looking forward to sharing life's adventures with him.

    • To be Mrs

      I was enjoying my bachelorhood thinking my life couldn't be better, until I met her and realized what I was missing. What started off as conversations about everything except dating, slowly but surely turned into something that would last a lifetime and beyond!

      Her genuineness, kindness, honesty, caring, creativity, curiosity, drive for knowledge, innocence, quirkiness, brings out the best in me.

       

      I can truly say I have found my 'partner in crime' in her...for this life, and next!
